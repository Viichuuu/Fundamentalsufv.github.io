I have chosen a career in Computer Engineering because everything related to technology has always caught my attention and this career allows me to train in many fields of technology. Furthermore, I believe that computing is an infinite source of information and hides many secrets and over time it is becoming something very important for our daily lives. I have chosen a degree in cybersecurity because it is a field that interests me a lot and I would like to dedicate myself to something related to it. I have chosen the bilingual degree because my intention is to work abroad, in addition to the fact that the great world power in this field is the United States and training in English can make things easier for me in the future.
