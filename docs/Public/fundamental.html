Computer engineering is a field of study that uses principles of computer science, computer systems engineering, information systems, software design, and networking to conceive, create, and care for computerized devices.
Computer engineers are responsible for envisioning and maintaining the digital tools we incorporate into our everyday lives, whether personal computers and smartphones or large company systems and essential infrastructure networks. An education in computer engineering commonly spans four years at colleges and tech schools. The coursework covers topics in mathematics, the natural world, engineering methodology, programming logic, and computer science.
